SkillsBuild – AI & NLP Basics

SkillsBuild offers beginner-friendly courses covering AI fundamentals, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and ethical considerations. The platform is perfect for those looking to explore AI concepts with structured guidance. Whether you're a student or a professional, this resource provides a strong foundation in artificial intelligence.

Great Learning – ML & NLP Certification Courses

Great Learning provides top-notch courses on machine learning, neural networks, and NLP, complete with certifications. Learn from industry experts and enhance your AI skills with practical applications. Ideal for beginners and professionals aiming to advance in the AI field.

Elements of AI – Free AI Course by University of Helsinki

Elements of AI Elements of AI is a free, university-backed AI course designed for beginners. It explains artificial intelligence concepts in a simple and engaging way, making it accessible to anyone curious about AI. This is a must-join course for AI enthusiasts looking for a solid start.

Google AI – Hands-on AI & ML Training

Google AI provides interactive tutorials on artificial intelligence and machine learning. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, this platform offers hands-on learning with practical applications. It's a great way to learn directly from Google's AI experts.

Coursera – AI for Everyone (Non-Technical Course)

Coursera AI for Everyone by Andrew Ng is a beginner-friendly, non-technical AI course. It covers AI concepts, business implications, and ethical considerations. Perfect for business professionals who want to understand AI without coding.

edX – AI & ML Courses from Top Universities

edX offers AI courses from globally recognized universities, with some free certification options. Learn machine learning, deep learning, and AI applications from world-class instructors. A valuable resource for students and professionals looking to upskill.

Udacity – Artificial Intelligence & Robotics Courses

Udacity AI School covers AI, robotics, and NLP with self-paced learning. The platform offers structured programs designed to make complex AI topics easy to understand. Ideal for anyone serious about an AI career.

CareerFoundry – Free Digital Marketing Course

CareerFoundry provides a beginner-friendly introduction to digital marketing. Learn SEO, content marketing, and online branding strategies. Perfect for business owners and marketers looking to enhance their skills.

Great Learning – Free Backend Development Courses

Great Learning offers free backend development courses with certification. Learn server-side programming, database management, and backend frameworks. A great resource for aspiring full-stack developers.

Codecademy – Web Development Courses & Tutorials

Codecademy provides interactive coding tutorials in web development. Learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React. Perfect for beginners starting their coding journey.

Great Learning – Free Full Stack Developer Course

Full Stack Developer Course teaches both frontend and backend development. The course includes hands-on projects and a certificate. Ideal for aspiring developers looking to build complete web applications.

Just Academy – Best Free Full Stack Development Courses

Just Academy compiles the best free courses for full-stack web development. Learn JavaScript, Node.js, and database management. A perfect resource for beginners and advanced learners alike.

Coursera – Full Stack Web Development Courses & Certificates

Coursera offers top-rated full-stack web development courses. Learn from institutions like Harvard, MIT, and Google with certification options. Great for anyone looking for a structured learning path.

Cursa – Free Online Web Development Courses + Certificate

Cursa provides free web development courses covering frontend and backend technologies. Earn a certificate upon completion. A fantastic option for self-paced learners.

CareerFoundry – SEO Training Course with Ahrefs

SEO Training offers an in-depth guide to search engine optimization (SEO). Learn the best practices from industry leaders and boost website rankings effectively. Ideal for digital marketers and content creators.
